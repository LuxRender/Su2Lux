SU2LUX, LuxRender exporter for SketchUp

version 0.45dev, 20 June 2015

by Abel Groenewolt, Alexander Smirnov, Luke Frisken, Martijn Berger and Mimmo Briganti

SU2LUX is partially based on SU2KT, SU2POV and Kojack's OGRE exporter and makes use of the jQuery library and the Farbtastic color picker.

LuxRender and SU2LUX are free software. Your questions and feedback are most welcome at the LuxRender forum.
